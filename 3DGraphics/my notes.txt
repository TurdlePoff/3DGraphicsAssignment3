Pursuit
- Similar to Seek but calculates a predicted position of the target based on it's current position and velocity

Evade
- Similar to Evade but avoids the target by avoiding it's predicted position

PathFollowing
- AI follows a set path of nodes by seeking for one after another
- In our case, if it reaches the last node, it seeks for the first node again

Crowd PathFollowing
- A crowd of AI following the set path of nodes through seek
