Controls:

Movement:
	[W] - Up
	[A] - Left 
	[S] - Down
	[D] - Right
	
Shooting:
	[SPACE] - To shoot
	
Navigating menu:
	Left Mouse click
	
Camera:
	[Scroll Mouse] click + drag to move point of view
	[ARROW KEYS] - to move position camera

PowerUps:
	Powerups grant invincibility for 5 seconds
	
---------------------------------------------------------------
	
Note: Skybox won't load with sprites so please press SPACE or move WASD after clicking start
